Project Idea

My Project: Create a QR tracker code for motorbike drivers and Taxi drivers to prevent stealing and murder.

The quick response tracker will contain information about the motorbikes and Taxis, such as the Vehicle Identification Number, the area it operates, the information of the owner, the driver or rider, and tarck the Taxi or motorbike when it is on the road and off.

Considering riding motorbikes and driving taxis is the primary income source for the young African generation. The idea of creating the QR tracker code for motorbike riders and taxi drivers is based on some worse situations in the past and still happening in some rural and urban areas in most African countries. Driving and riding overnight has been a problem and costs the life of some taxi drivers and motorbike riders in Africa. The idea is to store data of Taxis and motorbikes that will start using our QR tracker technology. Using our QR tracker will help us track and get the information as quickly as possible whenever they get a customer to take somewhere while on the road and when they have arrived at their destination.

The QR tracker code will be able to:

  • Scan the image of the customer,
  • Locate the local police station in the area,
  • Locate the motorbike or Taxi wherever it is,
  • The mileage it has done from the station,
  • Know when they are on the road and when they are off,
  • Get quick information as soon as possible,
  • In the situation of delay or missing, be able to alert the police and other security agencies.

The idea is of great significance as it will minimise the incidences of robbery and killings of drivers and riders. The police and the local community members have reported incidences of robbery and death of taxi drivers and motorbike riders. These incidences mostly happen at night when people will hire a Taxi or a motorbike thinking they are good customers, but at the end of their journey they will strangle the driver or rider and throw his body near the road or in the bush and rob his Taxi or his motorbike. The family will start looking for their loved one and report to the police the missing person; it could take hours or days to locate where the person is. It will be too difficult to locate the body of the missing person thrown in the bush, which can take days, but for those left near the road, it will be easy to find their bodies as the information will spread quickly. The system of QR tracker codes will help the drivers and the riders to carry on their daily activities with no fear and increase their income on a daily basis.

Motorbike riders in most cities of Africa, such as Dar es Salaam in Tanzania, scare to ride at night-time for fear of being robbed and killed. Some riders ride overnight in pairs, one motorbike will carry the customer, and the other one will follow them behind for security measures which cost financially as rather than using one motorbike, they use two where they could save money for fuel and service for the other motorbike. There are many awful situations of the murder of motorbike riders and taxi drivers, which have been reported to the police in several countries.
